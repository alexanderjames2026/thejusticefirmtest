Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

1. Introduction

Consumer Healthcare Compliances (“CHC,” “we,” “our,” or “us”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, shared, and protected when you visit our website or submit information through our forms.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following categories of information:

  • Name, phone number, email address, and contact details
  • Information related to potential legal claims
  • Device information, IP address, and location data
  • Website interaction and session activity
  • Marketing and communication preferences

3. How We Use Information

  • To evaluate potential case eligibility
  • To connect consumers with independent licensed attorneys
  • To respond to inquiries and provide requested services
  • To improve website performance and user experience
  • To comply with legal and regulatory obligations

4. Lead Certification & Consent Technologies

To document consumer consent and maintain compliance with applicable marketing and communication laws, CHC uses trusted third-party certification technologies including TrustedForm and Jornaya LeadiD.

These technologies may independently record website sessions, including page interactions, timestamps, IP address, and form submission activity for verification purposes.

  • TrustedForm certifies that a consumer provided consent to be contacted.
  • Jornaya (LeadiD) documents user journey data and interaction history to help verify authentic inquiries.

By submitting information through this website, you consent to the collection and storage of certification records by these providers.

5. Information Sharing

We may share submitted information with:

  • Independent licensed attorneys and law firms
  • Service providers assisting with case evaluation
  • Technology vendors supporting website operations
  • Entities required for legal or regulatory compliance

We do not sell personal information for unrelated third-party marketing.

6. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

Our website may use cookies, analytics tools, and tracking technologies to understand visitor behavior, improve services, and measure marketing performance.

7. Communication Consent (TCPA Disclosure)

By submitting a form, you agree that CHC and its partners may contact you via phone calls, text messages, or email using automated technology regarding your inquiry. Consent is not required as a condition of purchase or service.

8. Data Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards designed to protect personal information against unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse.

9. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to request access, correction, or deletion of your personal information. Requests may be submitted using the contact information below.

10. Third-Party Websites

Our website may link to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of external sites.

11. Children's Privacy

This website is not intended for individuals under the age of 18, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from minors.

12. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
